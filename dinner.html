Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pita bread, for serving
  • Optional: sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, and tahini sauce

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make a marinade.
  2. Add the chicken thighs to the bowl, ensuring they are fully coated in the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the chicken for 5-7 minutes on each side, until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  4. Remove the chicken from the heat and let it rest for 5 minutes. Slice into thin strips.
  5. Serve the chicken in pita bread, along with cucumbers, tomatoes, and a drizzle of tahini sauce, if desired. Enjoy!
