Moroccan Lamb Tagine

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs lamb shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 cup chicken or beef stock
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup dried apricots
  • 1/4 cup almonds, toasted
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large tagine or heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat. Brown the lamb chunks on all sides, then set aside.
  2. In the same pot, saut√© the onions until soft and translucent. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the cinnamon, turmeric, ginger, and paprika, cooking until fragrant.
  4. Return the lamb to the pot. Add the stock, diced tomatoes, dried apricots, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally, until the lamb is tender.
  6. Sprinkle with toasted almonds and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Enjoy with couscous or crusty bread!
