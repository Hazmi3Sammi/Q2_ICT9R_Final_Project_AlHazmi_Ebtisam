Knafeh

Ingredients:

  • 500g shredded kataifi pastry
  • 250g unsalted butter, melted
  • 500g sweet cheese or mozzarella, shredded
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp rose water
  • 1 tbsp orange blossom water
  • Ground pistachios for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the shredded kataifi pastry with melted butter until fully coated.
  3. Spread half of the pastry mixture into a greased baking dish, pressing it down firmly.
  4. Evenly spread the shredded cheese over the pastry layer.
  5. Cover the cheese with the remaining pastry mixture, pressing it down gently.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until golden brown.
  7. While baking, prepare the syrup by boiling sugar and water until slightly thickened. Stir in rose water and orange blossom water.
  8. Once the knafeh is baked, pour the syrup evenly over it while still hot.
  9. Garnish with ground pistachios and serve warm. Enjoy!
